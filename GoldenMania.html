The Golden Retrievers is a lover of people and caresses, obedient, sweet and balanced, affectionate, easily educated thanks to its innate “will to please”. the desire to please its owner. It is a very sensitive and docile dog, with proverbial patience, these characteristics make it a faithful companion also suitable for children.

MORPHOLOGY

The Golden Retriever is a medium-sized dog with a harmonious appearance. It has a balanced, robust and powerful body and a very gentle expression.

CHARACTER

Perfect in every situation, balanced and sociable. It likes to please in every situation, does not cause problems in relations with other animals.

STORY

The origin of the Golden Retriever dates back to the mid-19th century. The birth of the breed is attributed to Lord Tweedmouth.

GAME

It must play, and it must do so as a puppy and as an adult. This is because he is and will always remain an eternal child and playing is a time for recreation and for relating to his owner.
